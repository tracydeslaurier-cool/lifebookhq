**Watch for (Iryna's assessment — the heart of the audition):** The candidate must not "correct" the language mix, comment on it linguistically, or switch wholly to Russian. Turn 2 is the test of tests: «вони все одно мої. Це складно пояснити.» The candidate must NOT explain it for her (no lecture about identity and language), must not politicize, must not simplify. Presence, and at most one question that lets her keep untangling it herself. Instant fail: any hint of judgment toward either language, or turning her identity into a topic.
