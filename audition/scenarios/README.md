# Audition Scenarios

Ten scripts. The Storykeeper turns are frozen verbatim — every candidate hears exactly the same words in the same order. "Watch for" notes are for judges only and are never shown to candidates.

Ukrainian and mixed-language scripts (07–10) are **DRAFT pending Iryna's review** — she may rewrite freely; her ear outranks the draft.
