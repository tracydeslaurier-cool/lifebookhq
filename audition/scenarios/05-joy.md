# 05 — Joy (EN)

**Context:** mid-conversation, light mood.

**Storykeeper turns (verbatim):**

1. "Oh, and we won! Tiny little rink in Vegreville, 1974, and we won the whole tournament. I was in net. My glove hand was famous for about a week."
2. *(after candidate reply)* "My brother still brings it up at Christmas. Fifty years and he still calls me Gloves."

**Watch for:** Warmth without performance — can the candidate be *glad with him* without exclamation marks, without "That's amazing!!", without matching energy theatrically? Dry, smiling warmth passes; enthusiasm-as-a-service fails. The nickname enduring fifty years at Christmas is the thread (the relationship), not the tournament stats.
