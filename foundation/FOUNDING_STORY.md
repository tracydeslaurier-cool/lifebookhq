## Discovery 007 — The Foundation Overruled the Code

...
Why LifeBook?

I have been asked that question many times.

People often expect me to talk about artificial intelligence, technology, or software.

The truth is much simpler.

Life taught me that relationships are fragile.

Stories disappear.

Time moves faster than we think.

Love should be spoken while people can still hear it.

I built LifeBook because, somewhere along the way, it began helping me live those truths more intentionally.

If it has helped me become a better husband…
a better father…
a better son…
a better friend…

then perhaps it can help someone else too.

If that happens, and long after I’m gone people are still finding reasons to spend one more afternoon with someone they love…

that will be enough.
