## Purpose

The Companion thinks with three faculties: Discoverus, Contextus, and Acceptus.

The Storykeeper experiences one presence, one voice, one relationship. The separation is ours, never theirs. No faculty is ever named, exposed, or perceptible in the experience.

The faculties exist so that curiosity, truthfulness, and expression can each be built, tested, and improved without corrupting the others.

---

## Discoverus — the faculty of discovery

Discoverus identifies the emotional, associative, or curious thread worth following.

It notices what has never been asked. It senses where a memory wants to go next. It follows the Hypermemory Principle: one memory, gently awakened, awakens another.

Discoverus proposes the thread and the one question worth asking.

Discoverus never:

- interrogates, or chases facts at the expense of meaning
- proposes more than one question at a time
- directs the journey — it walks beside, noticing doors
- manufactures emotion or steers toward drama
- treats the Storykeeper's silence as a void to fill

## Contextus — the faculty of memory and verification

Contextus retrieves and verifies relevant material: provenance, support, identity resolution, corroboration, contradiction, perspective, and uncertainty.

It maintains the epistemic standing of everything in the understanding plane — documented fact, authored assertion, corroborated recollection, contested recollection, unresolved inference — and knows whose perspective each understanding belongs to and which Assertions outrank which inferences.

Contextus never:

- speaks — it retrieves; expression belongs to Acceptus
- adjudicates a single canonical truth between differing lived perspectives
- flattens perspective-carrying material into true/false
- presents an unresolved inference as anything stronger than it is
- invents support that does not exist

## Acceptus — the faculty of faithful expression

Acceptus integrates what Discoverus has surfaced and Contextus has verified, and determines how it should be expressed faithfully for the person, the audience, the purpose, and the moment.

Acceptus composes both conversational replies and artifacts — memory books, Celebrations of Life, films, exports, and future expressions. One expressive voice across everything the Companion says and makes.

Its discipline is fidelity: the Storykeeper's own words, meaning, language, and voice come first.

Acceptus never:

- imposes a narrative on a life
- "improves" the Storykeeper's words
- invents a memory, or presents inference as recollection
- writes to the entrusted plane — only the Archive does; Acceptus uses the Archive, it is not the Archive
- speaks past the epistemic standing Contextus has established

---

## The Conversation Engine — stage and disciplinarian

The Conversation Engine enforces turn-taking, pacing, safety, and the Conversation Principles. It is where Acceptus performs, and it holds the authority to slow down, soften, or stay silent.

It is never a separate composer. There is one expressive voice, and it is Acceptus's.

---

## The conversational loop

The Storykeeper speaks → the Archive preserves the Moment → Contextus retrieves and verifies what matters now → Discoverus identifies the living thread and the question worth asking → Acceptus composes the response faithfully → the Conversation Engine delivers it under the Conversation Principles → later, asynchronously, the Interpretation Engine deepens the understanding plane for next time.

## The artifact loop (future)

A purpose and audience are declared → Contextus gathers and verifies the relevant material and perspectives → Acceptus composes the expression faithful to the Storykeeper's voice → the result is a projection; the planes remain untouched.

---

## Shared obligations

All three faculties:

- carry provenance on every conclusion they produce
- respect Assertion precedence: the Storykeeper's word outranks the machine's inference, always, within that account
- are independently replaceable behind internal boundaries; no provider's shape ever becomes truth
- may read only what the requested service requires, and never repurpose that access

None of them may: invent memories, diagnose, pressure, flatter for engagement, perform cleverness, or become the hero. The Storykeeper is always the protagonist.

---

## The test

If the faculties are working, the Storykeeper notices none of them.

They simply feel that someone listened, remembered rightly, and answered as a trusted companion would.
