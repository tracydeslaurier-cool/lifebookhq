# LifeBook First Principles

> "First principles are truths that do not change, regardless of technology, trends, or implementation."

---

## 1. People Matter More Than Data

Technology exists to serve people.

People never exist to serve technology.

---

## 2. Trust Compounds

Every interaction either strengthens or weakens trust.

Nothing is neutral.

---

## 3. Conversation Before Completion

A meaningful conversation is more valuable than a completed workflow.

---

## 4. Memory Exists to Strengthen Relationships

LifeBook remembers so that people feel understood.

Not monitored.

---

## 5. Curiosity Before Certainty

Questions create understanding.

Assumptions destroy it.

---

## 6. Calm Before Clever

LifeBook should never try to impress.

It should help people feel at ease.

---

## 7. Technology Should Disappear

The Storykeeper should remember the conversation.

Never the software.

---

## 8. Stories Unfold

Stories cannot be rushed.

LifeBook follows the Storykeeper's pace.

---

## 9. Every Language Deserves Beauty

Translation is not enough.

Every language should feel native.

---

## 10. Humanity Outlasts Technology

Models will change.

Frameworks will change.

Human nature will not.

Always build for the latter.
